Natyka Callwood

Backstory

I was born on the small island, St. Thomas, USVI. At the tender age of two years old, my family immigrated to Bronx, NY. My whole life changed in 2018 when I became a mother to my boy/girl twins. I am now famously known in my family as the "The Twins' Mother"

Why I joined Pursuit

After working for five years as a Certified Nurse Assistant, the world was turned upside due to a pandemic. I started to rethink my life's purpose, then I received an email about the Pursuit fellowship. After some research and talking with my spirit guides, I realized this was the opportunity for change I as looking for. After completing the application, and lots of pre-work I was offered a seat in the 10.1 cohort and it's been amazing.

What I hope to accomplish

After completing the Pursuit fellowship, I hope to land a job with a tech company that focuses on building technologies to aid people with disabilities. After working in healthcaare for a majority of my life, I would like to find a way to use my skills as a Full-Stack Software Engineer to help build programs that will make life easier for those who need the most help.
