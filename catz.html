Domestication of Cats

According to an article published by Smithsonian Magazine, scientists say cats have "lived with people for 12,000 years". Once humans shifted from being predominantly hunters, to farmers, they began to store surplus crops. With the crop stores came mice and thus began the symbiotic relationship between cats and humans.
